Our Promise: We Know Nothing.

We built LockIn on a Zero-Knowledge architecture. Your Master Password is your key, and yours alone. It never leaves your device. We only store encrypted "gibberish" that no one—not even us—can decipher without your key.

This means your data is safe, even if our servers are compromised.
